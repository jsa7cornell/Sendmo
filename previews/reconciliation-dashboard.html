The reconcile identity (money columns, left to right):
Paid − Stripe fee − Refund to customer + Adjustment collected − Chargeback − Label cost + Refund from EasyPost − Adjustment charged = Net margin
A row is ✓ Reconciled when every counterpart movement is present and the math closes to the cent. Click any shipment ID for the full detail view — parties, addresses, package, the complete event-by-event ledger, and links out to EasyPost / Stripe / the tracking page.

Timeline — label created, carrier ship date (first scan), and delivery. Voided shipments never ship, so those cells read "— voided".
Carrier adjustments have two columns — Adjustment charged (what EasyPost billed the wallet) vs Adjustment collected (what we re-charged the customer, incl. the $1 handling fee). ≤ $1 absorbed · $1–$10 auto-recharged · > $10 flagged.
Chargeback — disputed amount clawed back plus the ~$15 Stripe dispute fee, as one figure. COMP shipments have no customer side.
