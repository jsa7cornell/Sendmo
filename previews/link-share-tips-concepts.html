Marketplace snippet — copy alternates

Goal: accurate (seller pays, not "free"), short enough to paste into a Marketplace listing without breaking the seller's own description, and clear about what the buyer does.

A — current (in mockups above)📦 I ship with SendMo — open my link, enter your address, and print the prepaid label. I cover shipping. sendmo.co/s/fqaYPCvYWS
B — friendlier📦 Shipping handled — I use SendMo. Once we agree on a price, just open my link, enter your address, and print the label. No shipping cost to you. sendmo.co/s/fqaYPCvYWS
C — minimal📦 I ship with SendMo. After we agree on price, use this link to print your shipping label — I'll cover it. sendmo.co/s/fqaYPCvYWS
D — branded headline✅ Ships with SendMo I cover shipping. Open my link, enter your address, print the prepaid label. sendmo.co/s/fqaYPCvYWS

My pick: A. Shortest, leads with the verb the buyer cares about ("I ship"), avoids the "free" word, and the steps are concrete enough to feel real. D is a strong second if you want a logo-ish headline that's grep-able in a listing scroll.
